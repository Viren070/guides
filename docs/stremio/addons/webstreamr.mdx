<h3>Description</h3>

Webstreamr provides free HTTP streams from various streaming sites. It pulls from a multitide of different sites, including
foreign language specific sites, so it is a great source of free streams for content that may not be as popular, especially if you do not
use a debrid service.

It is also good for more niche content that, even with a debrid service, may not have many results on torrent addons.

<h3>Configuration</h3>

1. Click Configure above
2. Select the languages you want to see streams for
   - For English content, simply leave `Multi` selected.
3. Scroll to the bottom, ignoring the options after `Show errors`
4. Click `Install`.
