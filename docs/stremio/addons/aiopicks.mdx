<h3>Description</h3>

AIOpicks is a recommendation addon that uses your Trakt history to generate personalised picks.

This is a good option if you want recommendations without manually curating many lists.

<h3>Before you configure</h3>

- Enable Trakt scrobbling in Stremio first: [Trakt](/stremio/extras/trakt#enabling-trakt-scrobbling)
- Make sure your Trakt history has enough watched items for meaningful recommendations

<h3>Configuration</h3>

1. Click the `Configure` button above to open the configuration page.
