Some Hisense TVs use Android TV, while others use a custom OS known as Vidaa OS. TVs from the USA are generally Android TV.
For those with Android TV, you can simply install Stremio from the Google Play Store.

For those with Vidaa OS, you can follow the steps from Stremio's official [blog post](https://blog.stremio.com/installing-stremio-on-hisense-tv/) to
install Stremio on your TV.
