You can browse public lists on [MDBList Top Lists](https://mdblist.com/toplists/) or directly from curators.

Popular curator examples:

- [Gary](https://mdblist.com/lists/garycrawfordgc/)
- [Riz](https://mdblist.com/lists/rizreflects/)
- [Snoak](https://mdblist.com/lists/snoak/)

Copy the list URL/ID and add it in StremThru Lists configuration.

A common setup is:

- A few public MDBLists for broad discovery
- One or two personal MDBLists with your own strict filters
- Optional Trakt/AIOpicks for history-driven recommendations
